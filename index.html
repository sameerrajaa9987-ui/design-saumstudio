    Questions homeowners ask

    What does the package include?+

    Full-home interiors — design, materials, fittings and on-site execution: TV unit, wardrobes with loft, modular kitchen, false ceiling, full painting, electrical work, bathroom vanity and more. Final scope is confirmed after a free site visit.

    How is the final price decided?+

    The starting prices are indicative. Your final quote depends on your home's measurements, layout and material choices — shared transparently after a free site measurement, with no hidden costs.

    Do you handle execution or only design?+

    Both. Saum Studio is fully turnkey — we design and execute under one roof, so you deal with one accountable team from concept to handover.

    How long does it take?+

    Most homes are handed over in about 8 weeks from design sign-off, depending on scope and site conditions. We run quality checks at every stage.

    How are payments made?+

    In simple milestone-based stages tied to project progress, discussed clearly before any work begins — so you always know what you're paying for.

    Which areas do you serve?+

    Across Mumbai & MMR — including Andheri, Powai, Khar, Thane, Chembur, Borivali, Mira Road, Kharghar and nearby localities.
